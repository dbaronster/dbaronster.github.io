Prior to Passionada, James produced High Times' Potluck also acting as Line Producer, and UPM. Penned by Victor Colicchio (Summer of Sam) Potluck features Jason Isaacs and was shot in New York City. Mr. Scura co-produced Mixing Nia, starring Isaiah Washington and Karyn Parsons (Fresh Prince of Bel Air) and began his producing career as the Executive Producer of the New York independent film Loose Women.

James wrote and produced The Titanic Chronicle, a historical documentary with David McCallum, Tim Curry, Marilu Henner, and Cloris Leachman.James has produced numerous industrials, videos, and commercial including work for Pepsi, Nabisco, Spaulding/Top-Flight, SoBe, Panasonic, and Lipton, Mr. Scura is a media consultant for the International Rescue Committee (IRC), a non-profit organization specializing in the relief of children.
