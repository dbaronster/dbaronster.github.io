Steve Jermanok, Writer
Steven Jermanok is an award-winning travel and feature writer. He has travelled to over 60 countries and written for Men's Journal, Travel & Leisure, Outside, Art & Antiques and The Boston Globe. Jermanok also penned HIGH NOON, a screenplay commissioned by Just Betzer (BABETTE'S FEAST). A graduate of the University of Michigan, Jermanok is the author of "New England Seacoast Adventures" and "Frommer's Great Outdoor Guide to New England." He currently resides in Boston with his wife and two kids.
