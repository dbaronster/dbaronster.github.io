Veteran screen star Theresa Russell began her film career as Robert Mitchum's daughter in THE LAST TYCOON. Russell then landed the female lead opposite Dustin Hoffman in Ulu Grosbard's STRAIGHT TIME. Most known for her portrayal of a serial killer in BLACK WIDOW, she has worked with Steven Soderbergh, Ken Russell and Nicolas Roeg. She was most recently seen in THE BELIEVER.
