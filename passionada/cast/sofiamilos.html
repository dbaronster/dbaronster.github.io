Sofia Milos is presently seen co-starring opposite David Caruso on "CSI:Miami" as detective Yelena Salas. Of Italian decent, she started as a model at the age of 15. She was first noticed on stage at the Beverly Hills Playhouse. On television, she's been the love interest to Matthew Perry on "Friends" and a series regular on "Caroline in the City". She also appeared on "ER", "The Twilight Zone", "Mad About You", among many others. Milos won acclaim with her portrayal of Annalisa Zucca, a tough mob boss up against Tony Soprano, on HBO's "The Sopranos". She has also had recurring roles in "Curb Your Enthusiasm" and ABC's series "Thieves". Other film work includes THE LADIES MAN and THE ORDER.
