Born Detoit Michigan, Seymour has been in film since 1959, when he starred in his first feature role in SHADOWS. He has since gone on to star in over 100 feature films. His most acclaimed roles include Chet in FACES for which he received an Academy Award nomination and Seymour Moskowitz in John Cassavetes' MINNIE & MOSKOWITZ. Other films include DICK TRACY, RUSHMORE and STEALING HARVARD.
