Lupe Ontiveros is well-known on stage, television and screen. Originally from El Paso, TX she began her career as an extra and eventually got her big break as La Nacha in the Oscar-nominated film EL NORTE. Her film credits include PICKING UP THE PIECES with Woody Allen, CHUCK AND BUCK, and AS GOOD AS IT GETS with Jack Nicholson. Most recently she starred in the award-winning film, REAL WOMEN HAVE CURVES for which she just received an Imagen Award nomination. She has also been seen in the TV series "Veronica's Closet" and on stage in Luis Valdez' Zoot Suit.
