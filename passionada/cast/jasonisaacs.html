Jason Isaacs was born in Liverpool, England, Isaacs has appeared in several highly acclaimed films such as BLACK HAWK DOWN, THE PATRIOT, and THE END OF THE AFFAIR. His most recent role as Lucius Malfoy in the HARRY POTTER series has earned him international attention from fans around the world. Upcoming projects include the roles of Captain Hook/Mr. Darling in P.J. Hogan's redux of PETER PAN, THE GOBLET OF FIRE - the fourth feature installment of J.K. Rowlings' HARRY POTTER books and Mike Figgis' HOTEL.
