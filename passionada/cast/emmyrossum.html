Emmy Rossum will be starring as Christine in the Phantom of the Opera slated for release next year. Rossum began her career at the Metropolitan Opera in La Boheme, Turandot and Hansel & Gretel. She went on to land guest starring roles and recurring roles on television series including "Law and Order" "As the World Turns" and "Snoops". She broke through playing a young Audrey Hepburn in ABC's Movie of the Week "Audrey Hepburn". Her film work began with a supporting role in IT HAD TO BE YOU, starring Nicolas Cage which led to a lead role in SONGCATCHER for which she was nominated for Best Newcomer at the 2001 Independent Spirit Awards. Rossum has just completed filming on Clint Eastwood's MYSTIC RIVER and Roland Emmerich's DAY AFTER TOMORROW. She can also be seen as the title character in NOLA, coming out this August.
