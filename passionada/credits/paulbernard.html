Mr. Bernard produced the soon-to-be-released High Times' Potluck, featuring Jason Isaacs. He produced and directed the independent feature Loose Women with Charlie Sheen and co-produced Mixing Nia, starring Isaiah Washington and Karyn Parsons. Paul was a producer on the digital feature Swimming Up Stream starring Michael Moriarty and Ben Savage, and on Leo, starring Joseph Fiennes, Elisabeth Shue, Sam Shepard, and Dennis Hopper.

Mr. Bernard has worked as an Assistant Director on The Patriot, Any Given Sunday, Three Kings, The Siege, Godzilla, Mission Impossible, Die Hard With A Vengeance, and Interview With A Vampire. In addition to his film experience, Paul was Line Producer and Production Manager for the Grateful Dead Summer Tour 92', Billy Joel PBS concert 93', and Pete Townsend Psycho Derelict 93'.
