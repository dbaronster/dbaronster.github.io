Jim Jermanok, Writer/Executive Producer
Jim Jermanok previously wrote and produced the short film, CLEAR CUT, starring Calista Flockhart and Stephen Fry. An accomplished theatre director and former agent, Jermanok attended Cornell University and the London School of Economics. He is currently producing THE LAST ILLUSION with Ben Kingsley and the independent films GIRDY, INSIDE OUT and RAM.

Steve Jermanok, Writer
Steven Jermanok is an award-winning travel and feature writer. He has visited over 60 countries and written for Men’s Journal, Travel & Leisure, Outside, Art & Antiques and The Boston Globe. Jermanok also penned HIGH NOON, a screenplay commissioned by Just Betzer (BABETTE’S FEAST). A graduate of the University of Michigan, Jermanok is the author of “New England Seacoast Adventures” and “Frommer’s Great Outdoor Guide to New England.” He currently resides in Boston with his wife and two kids.
